About This Project

Understanding the purpose, objectives, and impact of GovHealth Monitor

Problem Statement

Rural communities in India face severe challenges in accessing government hospital resources. People travel long distances only to find beds, ICU slots, or oxygen cylinders unavailable. Elderly and poor patients have no way to check availability before visiting. Emergency assistance for senior citizens is also extremely limited in rural areas, leaving many without timely care during critical situations.

Objectives

  • Provide real-time visibility of hospital bed and resource availability
  • Enable elderly citizens to send emergency SOS requests digitally
  • Inform poor and BPL families about government healthcare schemes
  • Reduce unnecessary hospital visits by enabling pre-verification
  • Empower hospital administrators with easy update tools
  • Create a simple, accessible interface usable by rural populations

Motivation

Inspired by the challenges faced during COVID-19 when patients and families desperately searched for available hospital beds, oxygen cylinders, and ICU slots. Rural and elderly populations were the worst affected. This system aims to prevent such helplessness by putting real-time information in the hands of every citizen, regardless of their technical literacy.

Advantages

  • Saves time and travel for rural patients
  • Large, simple UI accessible to elderly users
  • Bilingual support (English + Telugu)
  • Zero-cost emergency SOS for senior citizens
  • Centralized government scheme information
  • Real-time data with auto-refresh
  • Admin panel for easy resource management
