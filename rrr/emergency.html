Emergency Tips

Keep Helplines Ready

Save 108 (Ambulance) and 104 (Health) on your phone. These are free 24/7 emergency services.

Know Your Location

Always know your village/area name and nearest landmark. This helps emergency teams reach you faster.

Keep Documents Handy

Keep Aadhaar card and health cards ready. Government hospitals require these for free treatment.
